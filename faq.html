FAQ's

What's HoppIn All About?

HoppIn is Tonight’s Going Out App.We uncover the best events every day so you can get out and Live Your City. Get the lowdown on cool dates, hidden culture, epic parties and where to hang with friends. Discover, book, go – it’s that simple. Flash your HoppIn ticket or Scan the QR Code and you’re in.

Our curators work tirelessly around the clock to bring you the best events in your hood. Some last minute, some in advance. Our editors then pick out the hottest of the lot, while HoppIn’s computer wizardry personalises it all to you – so you can get your social life sorted in a flash, whatever you’re craving. Keep an eye out for regular perks such as access to sold-out shows, invitation-only events, freebies and discounts.


Can I Use HoppIn On My Smartphone, Tablet Or Computer?

Naturally! The HoppIn app is available on smartphones and tablets, and you can always book through the website.


BOOKING WITH HOPPIN

How Do I Book An Event?

Find something you like on the app or website, hit “Get Tickets”, select a date, ticket type and the number of tickets, and then confirm your booking. If it's your first time booking with us, you'll need to add your payment details here. But that's it. Easy peasy.


How Do I Know That My Booking Is Confirmed?

After booking, a ticket will appear on the app or website with the details of your booking. We’ll also send you a confirmation email, and you can always access all your tickets via "My Tickets".


How Do I Use A Credit Code?

If you've been lucky enough to find a code to get money off, be sure to enter it before booking. On the app, go to "Earn Credit" in your profile, tap “Redeem A Code”, enter your code, and – like magic – it’ll get used automatically when you book your next event. Enjoy! Please note, however, that you can only receive credit from one referral code.
You can earn additional credit by referring friends to HoppIn. Use your personal code also found under “Earn Credit” to throw them £5 – and then when they make a cash booking, you get £2. Sweet.


Do Events Sell Out?

They sure do, so it’s best to snap ‘em up quick. Once an event is sold out, a waiting list will be available – just hit the button to register your interest in any further tickets. We'll always try our hardest to snag some more for you, but it's not guaranteed.


How Late Can I Book An Event?

If you can see it on HoppIn, it's yours for the taking. We'll keep all tickets open until the last possible moment – we’re a bit spontaneous like that.


Can I Choose My Own Seats?

It isn't possible to book specific seats. Yet. In order to get you the best available seats, they’re allocated by the venue shortly before you collect tickets. Many of our events provide the option to choose your seating area or ticket type, in which case you’ll be allocated the best available seat in your selected seating area.


How Is Payment Taken?

Once you have a valid payment method on your account, you can buy any event in just a couple of steps. We accept PayPal, Apple Pay, American Express, Visa, MasterCard and Maestro. Your details are safely stored, so there’s no need to re-enter details on future purchases.


How Do I Change Payment Details?

To change your payment details on the app, go to “Payment Method” under “Settings”. Unfortunately you cannot yet change your card details on the website, and when you make a booking the latest added payment method will be used. If for whatever reason you can’t change your payment details on the app, feel free to get in touch with customer service and we’ll be able to help.


Is My Money Safe?

Totally. HoppIn is certified by STAR (Society of Ticket Agents and Retailers), an official ticketing industry regulatory body, so you can be sure that you’re booking from a trusted company. We encrypt your payment details using the industry’s leading standard, SSL (Secure Sockets Layer), and never store card information on your phone. That’s why you only see the last four digits.


Are There Any Hidden Fees?

Nope. None. Nada. Zilch. We’re big on being as transparent as possible, and we think it sucks to sneak in fees and charges just before you confirm your booking. At HoppIn, you pay what you see. Always.


If My Friends And I Book Separately, Will We Be Seated Together?

If all tickets are booked under one name, you’ll automatically be seated together. After booking, you can also share tickets with your fellow event goers if they have a HoppIn account. If you booked tickets using separate accounts, please call our customer support team and we’ll try to our best to sit you together.
Please be aware that for some shows, especially popular shows at the West End, this is not always possible if you book tickets separately.
